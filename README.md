# 📊 Power BI Sales Analysis Dashboard

This project is part of my Power BI journey with **Qafza AI Community**, where I focused on applying data analysis concepts to real-world business scenarios.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

The goal of this project is to analyze sales and profit data and transform raw datasets into clear, interactive insights that support business decision-making.

---

## 📌 Problem

Organizations often struggle with:

- ❌ Understanding profit distribution across product categories  
- ❌ Identifying high- and low-performing sub-categories  
- ❌ Tracking sales performance across different regions  
- ❌ Making data-driven decisions due to lack of clear visualization  

---

## 💡 Solution

Built an interactive **Power BI dashboard** that provides:

- 📊 Clear breakdown of **Sales and Profit by Category**  
- 📈 Detailed analysis of **Profit by Sub-Category**  
- 🗺️ Interactive **geographical visualization** of sales distribution  
- 🎯 Dynamic filtering to explore insights efficiently  

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

- Sum of Sales by Category (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies)  
- Sum of Profit by Category  
- Profit analysis by Sub-Category  
- Interactive Map showing sales distribution across regions  
- Filters and slicers for dynamic data exploration  
- Tooltips for deeper insights  

---

## 📊 Insights & Impact

- Identified high-performing and low-performing product segments  
- Enabled better understanding of profitability drivers  
- Improved ability to make **data-driven business decisions**  
- Transformed raw data into actionable insights  

---

## 🧠 What I Learned

- Designing clean and professional dashboards  
- Choosing the right visualization for each data type  
- Converting raw data into meaningful insights  
- Applying geographical analysis using maps and tooltips
